GitHub is a mini social network that makes it 
really easy for live music lovers to track the gigs of
their favourite artists.



Artists can sign up and list their gigs. When adding a gig, 
they shpould specify 
the date/time,location and genre of the gig.



An artists should have a page called My upcoming gigs.
From there, they should be able to edit or remove an 
existing gig, or add another gig to the list.



User should be able to view upcoming gigs or search them
 be artist, genre or location. they should be able to view 
details of a gig and add it to their calendar.



Additionally, users should be able to follow their 
favourite artists. 
When they follow an artist, they should see the 
upcoming gigs of their favourite artists 
in the Gig Feed.   


Use cases 

1. A page for Artist to signup and List thier Gigs.( location/Time/Genre)
 
2. A page for Artist called My Upcoming Gigs, They should be able to Add/ Edit/Remove/existing or outdated Gig details

3. User should be able to search by artist and view details of their upcoming gigs by genre/location/time and also add them, to their calendar

4. Users should be able to follow their fovourite artist/see their upcoming Gigs in thier Gig Feed.
